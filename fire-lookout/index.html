About the Game

Fire Lookout: Abandoned Post is a first-person narrative-driven mystery game.

As a newly hired fire lookout stationed deep in Summit Ridge National Park, you spend your days exploring the atmospheric landscape and connecting with colleagues. As you settle in, you can't shake the feeling that something is off.

As you begin to act on that feeling, you start to uncover the truth, but at what cost?

Psychological Horror Mystery Driven Story Rich Open World Choices Matter Atmospheric First-Person

The Gameplay

First-person, slow-burn narrative exploration. You navigate a semi-open world, talk to people over the radio, and make choices that change what you learn. Clues are everywhere - you just have to pay attention.

Key Features

🌲

Atmospheric World

A large, interactable environment with a world that feels alive.

📻

Decision-Based Progression

Radio conversations with real choices that change what you uncover.

🗺️

Open-World Exploration

Navigate by landmark, read a physical map, and find your own way through the park.

🎭

Character Voice Acting

All characters are fully voiced. Amplifying the immersion and the suspense.

What to Expect

Fire Lookout: Abandoned Post is a story-first mystery game. The answers are out there, but you have to find them yourself. If you like lore-rich narratives with light mechanics and heavy atmosphere, this one is for you.

From the Developers

This game was roughly 2 years in the making, with breaks for national exams and other adversities along the way. For two high school students with a budget of roughly 120, we don't believe we could have made it any better. It's honestly amazing to us that it found the audience it did.

We hope you enjoy the game as much as we enjoyed creating it. If you have feedback or thoughts, we would love to hear them. We will continue updating the game for the foreseeable future until we believe it is complete and enjoyable for as many people as possible.

Thank you to everyone who has supported us.
